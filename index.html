This intrigue started when my parents allowed me to use a computer and access the internet for the first time, I saw a youtube comment saying that if you type "do a barrel roll" in google something will happen. From then on, I wondered what other things could I type to trigger other easter eggs which eventually made me wonder how do those things work or how did people make it.
